本文分析了漢語七大方言區的地理分布特徵，探討了方言形成的地理因素。

This article analyzes the geographic distribution characteristics of the seven major Chinese dialect regions and explores the geographic factors in dialect formation.

<!-- more -->

## 摘要 | Abstract

漢語方言的分布與地理環境密切相關。本研究通過GIS技術分析了方言邊界與地形、河流等自然因素的關係。

The distribution of Chinese dialects is closely related to geographic environment. This study analyzes the relationship between dialect boundaries and natural factors such as terrain and rivers using GIS technology.

## 七大方言區 | Seven Major Dialect Regions

1. 官話 | Mandarin
2. 吳語 | Wu
3. 粵語 | Cantonese
4. 閩語 | Min
5. 客家話 | Hakka
6. 贛語 | Gan
7. 湘語 | Xiang
